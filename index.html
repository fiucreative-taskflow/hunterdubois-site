About

The Swiss Army Knife of Sports Creative

I'm Hunter DuBois — a creative director and full-spectrum storyteller based in Miami, FL. At Florida International University, I lead the creative vision for all 17 varsity athletic programs, and I built that operation from the ground up into one of the top creative departments in the Group of Five.

What sets me apart isn't one skill — it's all of them. I shoot the photo. I direct and edit the video. I run the social strategy. I design the graphics. I can walk into a shoot solo and deliver a campaign-ready product, or step back and lead a full-time staff and student pipeline to do it at scale.

I built VICE U from a concept into a nationally recognized brand and took FIU Football's Instagram from 108th nationally to Top 10 in back-to-back seasons. Numbers like that don't come from doing one thing well — they come from being able to do everything well, and knowing how to lead a team to do the same.
